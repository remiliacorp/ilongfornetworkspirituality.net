About This Show

June 11 - July 2

A collection of art from Remilia Collective will be presented at Hunters & Collectors, New Zealand and for sale as NFTs on this website.

Performances will be livestreamed at the opening on 5PM FRI 5-11-21
(Pacific Time: 10PM THURS, 5-10-21).

Buum will live debut his latest album, with guest performances from edolls and Salon & Cough.

An experimental realtime chat, Somachat, will be live during the course of the show for engagement between the artists and guests, and a continuous livestream of the show during its course.
