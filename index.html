About This Show

A collection of art from Wretched Worm, Atrpntime, Mara Barl, Fod Kom, Milady Sonora, Chensi Fang and Ilyena Neinel of Remilia Collective will be presented at Hunters & Collectors, Wellington, New Zealand.

The art will be for sale in an NFT auction on this website with a continuous livestream of the physical space running for the duration of the show.

An experimental realtime chat, Somachat, will be live on the website with two stations setup at the show to allow engagement between the artists and guests.
